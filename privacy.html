Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-17

"Lookinon Auto Post" ("the App") is an internal tool built and operated by our media business to automatically upload and schedule short-form videos to our own TikTok account(s). This policy explains what data the App accesses through the TikTok API and how that data is used.

1. Data We Access

Through TikTok's Content Posting API, the App accesses:

  • Basic profile information of the connected TikTok account (display name, avatar) needed to confirm which account is connected.
  • The video files, captions, and metadata that we ourselves upload through the App for publishing to TikTok.
  • Publish status information returned by TikTok (e.g. success, processing, failed) for the videos we submit.

2. How We Use the Data

Data accessed through the API is used solely to authenticate the connected account and to upload/schedule the videos we create ourselves. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing our own content.

3. Data Storage and Retention

OAuth tokens are stored securely on our own private servers and are used only to make authorized API calls on behalf of the connected account. Tokens are retained only as long as the App continues to be used for that account and are deleted upon disconnection or upon request.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any data obtained through the TikTok API with third parties. Data is used internally only, by our own team, for our own content publishing.

5. Your Rights

Since this App connects only accounts we own and operate, account owners may revoke access at any time via TikTok's account settings, or by contacting us using the information below to request deletion of stored tokens/data.

6. Contact
